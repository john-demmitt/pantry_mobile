For the monthly food distribution the approximate service area boundaries are the 118 Freeway to the north, Mulholland Drive to the south, the Ventura County line on the west, and Resida Boulevard on the East. More precisely, we service the following zip codes:

  • 90290 -- Topanga
  • 91301 -- Agoura Hills
  • 91302 -- Calabasas
  • 91303, 91304 -- Canoga Park, West Hills
  • 91306 -- Winnetka
  • 91307 -- West Hills
  • 91311 -- Chatsworth
  • 91324, 91325, 91326 -- Northridge
  • 91335 -- Reseda
  • 91356 -- Tarzana
  • 91364, 91367, 91371 -- Woodland Hills


Applicants must bring two pieces of identification to the Food Pantry: a photo ID and a mailed record of address (e.g., a utility bill received within the last 60 days) as proof of residence.
